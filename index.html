Our Rugby Program

Excellence, Discipline & Teamwork

A Tradition of Excellence

The Hoërskool Roodepoort Rugby program has a proud history dating back to the school's founding. Our program is built on the values of hard work, dedication, and respect for the game.

We strive to develop not only skilled rugby players but also young men of character who will make a positive impact in their communities long after they leave our fields.

Our coaching staff includes former provincial players and dedicated educators who understand both the technical aspects of the game and the importance of character development.

Our Philosophy

At Hoërskool Roodepoort, we believe that rugby is more than just a sport – it's a tool for teaching life skills, building character, and creating lasting bonds between teammates.

Message from Coaches

At Hoërskool Roodepoort Rugby, we are dedicated to developing exceptional players who embody our core values of respect, discipline, and excellence. Our program focuses on creating an environment where players can thrive both as athletes and as individuals.

This year, our first team has demonstrated remarkable skill and dedication, resulting in an impressive season record. Their hard work has earned them the opportunity to participate in a tour, where they will represent our school, our community, and our country.

The upcoming tour offers our players a once-in-a-lifetime experience to test their skills against provincial competition, learn from different styles of play, and grow as individuals through cultural exchange. These experiences will be invaluable to their development, both on and off the rugby field.

We are incredibly proud of what our players have achieved and the young men they are becoming.

— Roodies Coaches

Our Recent Achievements

A record of excellence on the field

League Winners 2024

In 2024 we were league winners. Throughout the season the u/15 rugby team scored a total of 735 points and only conceded 35 points.

Noordvaal Winners 2024

The u/15 rugby team won the Noordvaal tournament in 2024.

Jaru Tournament 7's Shield Winners

Our team displayed exceptional skill and teamwork to win the prestigious Jaru Tournament 7's Shield in 2024, demonstrating our dominance in the shorter format of the game.

League Semi Finals 2023

In 2023 we reached the semi-finals of the league, narrowly missing out on the finals.

Provincial Players

In the past few years the school had numerous players selected for The Golden Lions school teams.

Sports Excellence Award

Received the Gauteng Department of Education's Sports Excellence Award for comprehensive sports development.

Our 2025 Rugby Tour

A Five-Day Rugby Adventure

An Exciting Rugby Experience

From April 1-5, 2025, at Sundown Country Estate near Rustenburg, our rugby team will embark on an intensive five-day tour designed to challenge and develop our players both on and off the field as part of the Blikkies Groenewald Rugby Tournament.

This tour represents an excellent opportunity for our players to:

  • Compete against quality opposition from different regions
  • Develop their skills through specialised training sessions
  • Build stronger team bonds and camaraderie
  • Create lasting memories with teammates
  • Represent our school with pride

Tour Details

Cost: R4000 per player (all-inclusive package)

What's included:

  • Transportation to and from all destinations
  • Accommodation for four nights
  • All meals and refreshments
  • Match and training facility fees
  • Recovery sessions and team building activities
  • A custom tour jersey for each player
  • Tournament entry fees
  • Jersey washing during the tour

Tour Benefits

Previous tours have proven to be pivotal in player development, with many participants showing significant improvement in skill level, game awareness, and mental resilience. The tour provides a professional environment that prepares players for higher levels of competition.

Important Note: After this tour, a select team will be chosen to tour Ireland in December 2025 - an incredible opportunity for our top performers.
